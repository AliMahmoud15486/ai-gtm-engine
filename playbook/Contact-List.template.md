**Tips**
- One persona per segment to start (e.g. Founder/CEO for SMB).
- Write the `Personalization hook` from something *true and specific* about the brand — it's the biggest lever on reply rate.
- Reveal verified emails only right before sending (saves credits, avoids stale data).
- Respect privacy law for your target regions; honor opt-outs and suppression lists.
