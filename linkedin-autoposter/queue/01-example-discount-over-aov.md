"Run a discount, get more sales." True — but blanket monthly discounts quietly erode margin, because you also discount the customers who'd have paid full price.

A smarter play: discount over AOV. Trigger the offer only once an order crosses a target basket value ("spend X, get 15% off"). Now the discount grows average order value instead of shrinking your margin.

Same promo budget. Completely different P&L.
